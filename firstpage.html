About

I believe in forward-thinking design

I am hard-working, motivated, creative and enthusiastic web designer, who always seeks to achieve the highest outcome at a complex environment with a rapidly changing context of the 21st century.

I love being an integral part of the creative process and rising to the challenge of making complex software approachable to the masses. With all my work, I hope to empower users and simplify their everyday lives.

The only constant in this world is change, and that's especially evident in the way I design and the way I build. I am excited to see the challenges presented by new technologies and apply my skills toward solving the problems that come with those changes.

I believe that forward-thinking design can be a driving force behind technological progress. In the various projects I was a part of, I have worked to advance user experience. To stay current by staying ahead of the current. To infuse the simplest interactions with beauty and a sense of ergonomics.

I am accustomed to working under pressure in a challenging and fast-paced environment, particularly when dealing with multiple projects at the same time.

I envision each of my creations as a singular piece at the service of each client’s communication strategy while introducing elements of the avant-garde.

    • How do I handle a Project?

    1.Briefing (Free)

    Send me a detailed briefing includig the following information: who you are/ core values/ business goals/ target group/ what you are selling/ required features/ scope of the project or sitemap/ timeframe and deadlines/ examples of other sites you like/ everything else you think is important.

    2.Evaluatin (Free)

    I will have an in-depth look at your briefing and evaluate whether the project fits both parties conditions and requirements. If it's a match, we will have a meeting (in person or via Skype/Hangout/etc.) to discuss how to proceed.

    3. Workshops

    We discuss approaches and concepts in order to find the perfect solution for your needs. If necessary, there can be multiple workshops.

    4. Design

    This is where the magic happens. I will work on the Art Direction, Concept and Design. Usually I present a moodboard or 2-3 screens that show the look and feel of the final product. If required further screens will be designed (extra costs).

    5. Implementation

    After approval of the design/mood the respective screendesign will be developed. I will provide a test-link so you can check the current status after each milestone.

    6. Quality Assurance

    After developing all parts of the website we continue to test on multiple browsers and devices to fix bugs. Given your final approval the website is ready to be shipped.
